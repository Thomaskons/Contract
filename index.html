Recitals

WHEREAS, the Company is engaged in investment and trading activities;

WHEREAS, the Investor desires to invest capital with the Company, and the Company desires to accept such investment under the terms set forth herein;

WHEREAS, the Investor qualifies as an Accredited Investor and maintains an active Evertas Investment Insurance Policy covering the full investment amount;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

1. Investment Amount and Payment Terms

1.1 Investment Amount

The Investor agrees to invest a total amount of EUR 10,000 (ten thousand euros) (the "Investment").

1.2 Payment Method

The Investment shall be paid by wire transfer.

1.3 Payment Due Date

Payment shall be made within one (1) business day of signing this Agreement.

1.4 Purpose of Investment

The Investment shall be used exclusively for investment and trading activities conducted by the Company.

2. Investment Structure and Expected Returns

2.1 Nature of Investment

This Investment does not represent equity ownership, voting rights, or shares in the Company. The Investment is a capital allocation to be managed under the Company's proprietary trading strategies.

2.2 Expected Monthly Returns (Non-Guaranteed)

The Parties acknowledge that, based on the agreed trading strategy and normal market conditions, the trading account is projected to generate monthly returns in the range of 25% to 30%.

2.3 Estimated Monthly Profit

Based on an initial capital of EUR 10,000, the projected monthly profit is estimated at EUR 2,500 to EUR 3,000, assuming approximately four (4) trades per day over twenty (20) trading days per month.

2.4 Guarantee and Insurance Coverage

All invested capital and projected returns are guaranteed and insured under the Evertas Investment Insurance Policy. In the event of any loss due to trading activities or unforeseen events, Evertas Insurance Company shall indemnify the Investor up to the full insured sum, ensuring the safety of the Investment.

3. Representations and Warranties

3.1 By the Company

The Company represents and warrants that:

  • It is duly organized and authorized to enter into this Agreement;
  • It will manage the Investment in good faith and in accordance with professional investment standards;
  • All material information relevant to the Investment has been disclosed to the Investor.

3.2 By the Investor

The Investor represents and warrants that:

  • They qualify as an Accredited Investor under applicable laws;
  • The Investment funds are derived from lawful sources;
  • They understand the risks associated with trading and investment activities;
  • They are investing voluntarily and with full knowledge of potential outcomes.

4. Use of Funds and Reporting

4.1 Use of Funds

The Company shall use the Investment solely for trading, investment operations, and associated risk management activities.

4.2 Reporting

The Company shall provide monthly performance summaries and quarterly detailed reports upon request.

5.2 Scope of Coverage

The policy provides coverage against all perils, including but not limited to theft, fraud, cyber incidents, internal collusion, system malfunctions, market volatility, and economic disruptions, subject to exclusions and conditions outlined in the Master Policy wording.

5.3 Claims and Indemnification

In the event of a covered loss, Evertas Insurance Company shall indemnify the Investor up to the full insured sum. Valid claims shall be settled within 30 business days following receipt of satisfactory proof of loss.

6. Confidentiality and Non-Competition

6.1 Confidentiality

The Investor agrees to keep all proprietary, financial, and strategic information strictly confidential for a period of five (5) years following termination of this Agreement.

6.2 Non-Competition

The Investor agrees not to engage in competing investment or trading activities that directly replicate the Company's proprietary strategies for a period of two (2) years following termination.

7. Term, Termination, and Capital Return

7.1 Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in force unless terminated by mutual written consent or material breach.

7.2 Withdrawal and Capital Return

Upon termination, the Investor shall be entitled to the return of capital subject to open trading positions and applicable settlement periods.

7.3 Confidential Materials

Upon termination, all confidential materials shall be returned or destroyed.

8. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

8.1 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Switzerland.

8.2 Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration, seated in Zurich, Switzerland, in accordance with internationally recognized arbitration rules.

9. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, or agreements. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by both parties.

10. Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
